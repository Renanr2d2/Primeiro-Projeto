Spider-Man

Hi, my name is Peter Parker, but you can call me Parker. A few years ago I was bitten by a radioactive spider that altered my DNA and gave me incredible abilities.

Abilities such as superhuman strength, agility, and speed amplified, heightened reflexes and senses, and the ability to climb walls.

Today, I use these skills to do good and protect those in need, because one day, someone told me that with great power comes great responsibility.

So that's me, the neighborhood buddy.
